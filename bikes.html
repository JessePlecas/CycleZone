Bikes

Bikes for him, for her, for your commute, for the road, for the trail. Whatever your needs, CycleZone has you covered.
We have a selection of bicycles in store to suit your needs. If you are looking for a specific bike or would like one built just for you- come in and let us know we can get that sorted for you.

Giant is the world's leading brand of high-quality bicycles and cycling gear. Part of the Giant Group, which was founded in 1972, the brand combines craftsmanship, technology and innovative design. CycleZone are delighted to work with Giant and supply their bikes, if you have your eye on a particular Giant bike and would like us to order it, just give us a call.

Cube is a large Germanic bike company, held in high regard for quite some time due to producing incredibly well designed bikes at affordable prices. The reasons behind their great pricing is all their bikes are hand assembled in their Bavarian factory. Cube bikes have much better components than most others and offer an excellent ride. CycleZone have a collection of Cube bikes in store though if your chosen bike happens to not be in store we would be happy to order it in for you.

Liv is the sister to Giant, so you can be assured that quality is still there for every single design. The designers at Liv put women first, with every bike frame perfectly measured and assembled with the women and their comfort in mind. Liv ensure that all the specs are perfectly suited to the individual bike so that you don't have to. Cyclezone have a number of Liv bikes in store for you to look at and take home, though as always- if you have your eye on a particular Liv bike which is not in store just let us know and we'll have it ordered for you right away.

Dawes are a superb bicycle range offering a large selection of different styles. All bikes are machine mitred making their joints very accurate. The Dawes bicycle offers you the unique blend of heritage and amazing performance, the classic and heritage range are ideal for carrying extras in the front baskets. The Dawes hybrid bikes offer a modern design and sleek components. Dawes have upgraded their tyres with to include a reflective strip around the sides for improved visibility. CycleZone have a number of Dawes bikes in store for you to choose from- if your Dawes bike is not in the store just let us know what you're looking for and we'll get that ordered in for you.
