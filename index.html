Our Services

Bike to Work scheme

Using the Bike to Work scheme, your employer can help you obtain a brand new bike and safety equipment worth up to €1,250 for a regular bike, and up to €1,500 for an electric bike. The scheme covers your new bike and gear, so you can get moving in style and save up to 52% of the retail price of bike and equipment.
If your employer has yet to register, just send them here and you'll be a few clicks away from your shiny new bicycle.

Bike maintenance and repair

Not only do we sell a select range of bicycles here in our store, we also offer full bike maintenance and repair. We love to repair bikes and it's the biggest part of our business. With over 30 years experience between us fixing bikes, we now know a thing or two!
Not only can we fix them, we can build them. Here at CycleZone we understand that everyone is different and has different tastes and requirements in a bicycle- and we do love a challenge so you can design your own bike and we will build it for you, if you have a racing bike and require custom wheels let us know, we'll get that sorted for you no problem.
Call into our store in Blanchardstown to discuss this more, we're right on the main street with parking right outside and plenty of space to park and lock your bike!
